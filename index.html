Meet Your Broker

Hi, I'm Beth Van.

Owner-Broker & the heart behind Bon Realty since 1997.

I started Bon Realty because I believed Southwest Michigan deserved a real estate agency built on real relationships — not just transactions. Licensed since 1994, I'm a working broker who stays personally involved in every deal.

Most of my business comes from people who've worked with me before, or from their friends and family. That's the kind of trust I work hard to earn every day.

Whether you're buying your first home, selling a family property, or exploring waterfront options on one of Michigan's beautiful lakes — I'm here to guide you with honesty and care.
