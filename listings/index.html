Want full photo galleries embedded here? — IDX Setup Guide for Beth

An IDX (Internet Data Exchange) feed gives your website licensed access to full MLS listing data including all photos, updated automatically. Here's how to get it set up:

  1. Contact your MLS board (Southwest Michigan MLS / Michigan Regional MLS) and request IDX authorization for your website. This is standard — most boards approve it.
  2. Ask FlexMLS for your IDX embed credentials or sign up for a FlexMLS IDX widget plan. Alternatively, services like iHomeFinder or IDX Broker plug directly into FlexMLS.
  3. Once you have the embed code, replace the placeholder comment below in listings/index.html with your real IDX widget code — the listings and photos will appear automatically.

Replace the <iframe> above with your IDX widget embed code. It will look something like this:

<!-- PASTE YOUR IDX WIDGET CODE HERE --> <script src="https://your-idx-provider.com/widget.js" data-agent-id="YOUR_AGENT_ID"> </script>
